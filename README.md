# Customer Segmentation Analytics Platform

## Overview

The Customer Segmentation Analytics Platform is a machine learning solution designed to identify distinct customer groups based on purchasing behavior, demographics, and spending patterns.

The platform enables businesses to better understand customer behavior, improve marketing effectiveness, personalize campaigns, and optimize customer engagement strategies.

---

## Business Problem

Organizations often struggle to:

- Understand customer purchasing behavior
- Personalize marketing campaigns
- Allocate marketing budgets effectively
- Identify high-value customer groups
- Improve customer retention

Traditional marketing approaches treat all customers similarly, leading to lower engagement and inefficient spending.

This project applies clustering techniques to discover meaningful customer segments and generate actionable business insights.
